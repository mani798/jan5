Telangana palakoora is a spinach dish cooked with lentils eaten with steamed rice and rotis.
Peanuts are added as special attraction and in Karimnagar District, cashew nuts are added.
Popular Telangana curry dishes (known as Koora) include Boti (derived from mutton) and Phunti Koora made out of Red Sorrel leaves.
